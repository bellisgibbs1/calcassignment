Cost of Christmas Trees in April

By Briana Ellis-Gibbs

Spring is coming, and the leaves on trees and flowers are blooming. Nature is coming alive again. Consumers do not have to wait for Spring to buy Christmas trees because they are all year long. NYC NewsService decided to compare the cost of 6ft, 9ft, and 12ft Christmas trees sold at different retailers during April to see how much these trees cost when nature comes alive again.

Amazon sells the cheapest 6ft, 9ft, and 12ft Christmas trees compared to Home Depot, Micheals, and Walmart. A 6ft tree costs $29.99, a 9ft tree costs $99.99, and a 12ft tree costs $280.77 on Amazon. Micheals sells the most expensive 6ft Christmas tree, which costs 62.42. It costs more money to buy 9ft and 12ft trees at Walmart than at HomeDepot, Michaels, and Amazon.

As consumers begin to enjoy Spring again and leaves grow back on trees, consumers can also treat themselves to a Christmas tree year-round.
